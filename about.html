About This Dashboard

Clarity. Context. Control. Fund(a)Metrics turns raw data into confident decisions.

📊 Purpose

Fund(a)Metrics helps investors explore mutual fund performance with clarity and control. Compare peers, track returns, and uncover insights—without the noise.

🧠 Methodology

Metrics are sourced from verified fund disclosures and normalized for consistency. Peer comparisons highlight top performers with dynamic averages and intuitive formatting.

🎨 Design Ethos

Every layout is crafted for premium clarity—modular, responsive, and emotionally breathable. Tables, toggles, and cards are grouped with expressive intent.

🧰 Features

  • Chart/Table toggle for visual and tabular views
  • Category and fund filtering
  • Peer comparison with dynamic averages
  • Metric cards showing fund vs category
  • Ratios tab with risk-adjusted metrics

🚀 Vision

We're exploring percentile ranks, export options, and deeper insights to make the dashboard even more intelligent and emotionally confident.

👋 About Me

Hi, I’m Ram—a passionate investor and dashboard designer. I’m not a SEBI-registered financial advisor, and this dashboard is not intended as investment advice. It’s a personal project built out of curiosity, creativity, and a love for data storytelling.

Every chart, table, and toggle reflects my belief that financial tools should be clear, expressive, and empowering. If you find this helpful or have ideas to improve it, I’d love to hear from you.
